The Cat Math Do Story

When Oscar was 4, I (Dave) started teaching Oscar the alphabet and counting to Ten. One day it occured to me that Oscar didn't know the difference between numbers and letters. So, I set out to introduce Oscar to their differences. I told him that Math is how numbers play together.

Then, I told him that the math playground has five rides; plus, minus, times, divide, and equals. I then said that plus goes up and minus goes down.

It wasn't long after that I came up with the board game you're holding. We played it for over a year before I thought I should try to see if other kids would enjoy playing as much. I hope you do! :-)
